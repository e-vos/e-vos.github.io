My name is Elliot Vosburgh. I am a 2<sup>nd</sup> year graduate student in the [Master of Environmental Science and Management](https://web.uri.edu/cels/academics/mesm/remote-sensing-and-spatial-analysis/) program at the [University of Rhode Island](https://web.uri.edu/cels/).
{: style="text-align: justify"}

I have experience using ESRI products, ERDAS Imagine, QGIS, and R in geospatial applications. My personal interests in archeology and history happily overlap with my professional work, especially in the use of *Li*ght *D*etection *A*nd *R*anging (LiDAR) technology.
{: style="text-align: justify"}

This website contains my own writing on a variety of geospatial, environmental, and history topics.
{: style="text-align: justify"}
